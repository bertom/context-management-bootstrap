## Troubleshooting

[Replace with actual troubleshooting for YOUR PROJECT. For a webshop:]

### Common Issues

**Issue: Cannot add item to cart**

**Possible Causes:**
- Item is out of stock
- Browser cookies disabled
- Session expired

**Solutions:**
1. Check product availability
2. Enable cookies in browser settings
3. Refresh page and try again
4. Clear browser cache and cookies
5. Try a different browser

**Issue: Payment fails at checkout**

**Possible Causes:**
- Incorrect card information
- Insufficient funds
- Card expired
- Payment gateway issue

**Solutions:**
1. Verify card number, expiry date, and CVV
2. Check account balance
3. Try a different payment method
4. Contact customer support with error message
5. Check if card is blocked by bank

**Issue: Order confirmation email not received**

**Possible Causes:**
- Email went to spam folder
- Incorrect email address
- Email delivery delay

**Solutions:**
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Verify email address in account settings
3. Wait 10-15 minutes for delivery
4. Check order status in account dashboard
5. Contact customer support if still missing
